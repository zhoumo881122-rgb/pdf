Frequently asked questions

Do I need to register?

No. VagaPDF is built for direct use without login or registration.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes. The layout is responsive and works on desktop, tablet and mobile browsers.

Why are tools client-side?

Client-side processing keeps simple PDF tasks fast and avoids sending selected files to a VagaPDF upload server.

Privacy first

Most tools run locally in your browser with client-side JavaScript. Your selected files are not uploaded to a VagaPDF server by this static website.

How it works

Pick a tool, add your files, adjust options and download the result. For very large files, keep the tab open while the browser finishes processing.
